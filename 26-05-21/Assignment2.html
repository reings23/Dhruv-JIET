Mukesh Dhirubhai Ambani is an Indian billionaire businessman, and the chairman, managing director, and largest shareholder of Reliance Industries Ltd. (RIL), a Fortune Global 500 company and India's most valuable company by market value. According to Forbes, he is the richest person in Asia with a net worth of US$ 77.5 billion and the 10th richest person in the world, as of 22th May 2021.
Gautam Shantilal Adani is an Indian billionaire industrialist who is the chairman and founder of the Adani Group -- an Ahmedabad-based multinational conglomerate involved in port development and operations in India.Adani is the president of the Adani Foundation, which is primarily led by his wife, Priti Adani. According to Forbes, his family's net worth is estimated to be around US$69.0 billion as of 22 May 2021. He is the second richest man in Asia and 15th richest man in the world according to Forbes, a spot attained in 2021
Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and a former chairman of Tata Sons. He was also chairman of Tata Group, from 1990 to 2012, and again, as interim chairman, from October 2016 through February 2017, and continues to head its charitable trusts.He is the recipient of two of the highest civilian awards of India, the Padma Vibhushan (2008) and Padma Bhushan (2000).
Azim Hashim Premji is an Indian business tycoon, investor, engineer, and philanthropist, who was the chairman of Wipro Limited.Premji remains a non-executive member of the board and founder chairman. He is informally known as the Czar of the Indian IT Industry. He was named the third richest person in India with an estimated net worth of US$31.3 billion according to Bloomberg Billionaires Index. In 2013, he agreed to give away at least half of his wealth by signing The Giving Pledge. Premji started with a $2.2 billion donation to the Azim Premji Foundation, focused on education in India.
